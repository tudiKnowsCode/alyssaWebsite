My story

Passionate About Every Child's Potential

Hi, I'm Alyssa — a certified special education teacher and learning specialist with over 7 years of experience working with children who learn differently. My passion is helping students build confidence and discover that learning can actually feel good.

I've had the privilege of working with children across a wide range of learning profiles — including dyslexia, ADHD, processing disorders, and other challenges that make the traditional classroom feel like an uphill battle. I know how hard these kids work every single day, and I'm here to make sure that hard work pays off.

Beyond working directly with students, I'm deeply committed to supporting families. Navigating the special education system — IEPs, 504s, evaluations, school meetings — can feel overwhelming. My goal is to be your guide, your advocate, and your partner throughout that process.
